E-mails links and target attribute

In this page, you will see examples about e-mail link and an usage of target attribute with links. E-mails links are used to send a message to a specified e-mail address, opening an e-mail client installed in the computer. The target attribute is used to determine where pages will be displayed when an user clicks on a link.
